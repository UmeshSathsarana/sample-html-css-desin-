About the Class

Mr. Priyantha Wickramasinghe

Fourteen years of teaching A/L Accounting to Grade 12 and 13 students across Colombo, with a syllabus built directly from examiner reports rather than assumptions. Every topic is taught the way it's actually asked — not the way it's usually taught.

Classes run in small batches so every student's answer scripts are checked personally, every week, with written feedback — the same way an audit would flag an error.

B.Com (Special) — Univ. of Colombo ACA — ICASL Former Visiting Lecturer, Aquinas Author, "Ledger Logic" Series
